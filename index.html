About Me!

Hi! My name is Kyle Oden. I am a graduate student at the Pratt Institute in New York and am studying Information Experience Design. Originally, I am from Inglewood, California, which is right in the middle of the Los Angeles County. I currently live in the Upper West Side and am enjoying learning while making new friends and exploring the City!

I have a bachelors in Cognitive Science from the University of Pennslyvania, where I was a 4 year member of the DI track & field team ( I was a short distance sprinter! ). In terms of hobbies I enjoy watching anime, coding a little on the side, and nice evenings in Central Park!
